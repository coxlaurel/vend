Wilder Brown

Mechanical Engineer

Hi I’m Wilder, I love woodworking and being outside. If I were a vending machine snack I would want to be Swedish fish and friends, the little turtles are so cute! I am a MechE, and for this project I wanted to develop my fabrication skills to work with new materials, tools, and design more complex parts to tighter tolerances. I also wanted to use design skills to make the project look good visually, help manage the project to be well scoped and functional at the end, and help create a positive teaming experience as well as a project that meets all of our technical expectations.

Most importantly, I had a fantastic teaming experience and I believe I helped foster a supportive, friendly, and productive team environment.
